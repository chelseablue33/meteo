\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97Thought process\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\
\
		The design I started with on this App is based on a couple of concepts. \
\
		1) To create a weather app that would help people work with the weather to plan their activities.\
\
		Application: Each subscriber would enter is favorite activities ( Arts, Food, Sports and Family ) and would receive a weather-sensitive personally 		curated activity schedule based on the upcoming weather forecast. No more getting rained on on BBQ day!\
\
		2) To design a weather app that would help make the weather less ominous.\
\
		Application: Weather and traffic are often anticipated with dread because of their unavoidable nature. But the Traffic app Waze has managed to 		make traffic dreadful with its simple and cartoonish User Interface. Similarly, the design of my weather is meant to help remove the seriousness 		often attached with the weather by applying playful and light design principles, from: light colors, cartoonish icons, rounded typography and 		playful copy content. \
\
\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97-Trade-offs and Wishlist\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\'97\
\
		The \'93Dial\'94 to display the current day\'92s hourly weather was a lot more work than I anticipated and had to be simplified from the original concept, 		which was a touch-and-drag gestural control. \
		With more time I also would have made each activity button display more specific recommendations.\
		I don\'92t have much experience with Swift and I had to learn a lot and get a fair amount of help to understand how to retrieve to 5-day forecast\
		from the OpenweatherMap forecast API.\
